Introduction

Welcome to my internship report blog. This section outlines my professional experience at Edumotiv, located in Lyon.

Duration: My internship lasted from June 9th 2025 to August 1st 2025.

Why This Company? I chose Edumotiv because their project aligned very well with my career goals. My objectives were to get hands-on with a real projects and to surpass myself with very specific demands and task from real clients.

Through this experience, I aimed to develop my ability to make well constructed scripts and feature in low time while debugging new and older features, understand and improve core systems in a meaningful way, all while thinking my code to as malleable as possible, and lastly to contribute meaningfully to the organization.
