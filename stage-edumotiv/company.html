Presentation of the Company and Market

Company Story: Founded in 2011, the company began as a service provider for businesses, offering project consulting for SMEs. It quickly became the first reseller for Odoo. In 2013, it developed the innovative Edumotic learning method. By 2014, the company returned to its roots, providing SAP consulting services, and from 2014 to 2018, it served as an SAP consultant and trainer. In 2018, it established a training center based on EPSI's methodology. The company achieved a turnover of €1 million in 2022 and, by 2024, expanded into IT services with Internow.

Company Overview: Edumotiv is a medium-sized enterprise specializing in giving formations in multiple domains (e.g. SAP, AI, etc...).

Market Positioning: The company targets SME and big enterprises. The industry is trending toward quality courses in various domains. The market position is unknown.

Organisation and Workforce: Edumotiv is led by directors Tatiana Gain and Alexandre Gain. It has a core team of 6 employees, supported by a network of 15 trainers.

Complementary Infos: Operating exclusively in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region, the company faces declining demand amid an oversaturated market, leading to intense competition on price, quality, and delivery times. The company emphasizes quality as its core strength. Its differentiation lies in a unique pedagogical method and expertise in SAP software.
