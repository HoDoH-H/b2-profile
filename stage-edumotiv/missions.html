Missions and Activities

Responsibilities

During my internship, I was responsible for creating and implementing new features, such as making a simple character controller for vr and interactions (e.g. doors, light switches, etc), as well as debugging and improving older scripts. My role evolved, allowing me to take on more complex tasks such as completely remaking older core systems (e.g. Database Manager, Game Manager, Special Events), creating advanced features (e.g. Time System, Weather System) or even optimisations (e.g. Mesh optimisation, etc... The project went from ~55fps to ~65/70fps).

Tools and Methods

I used Azure Dev Ops to store the project (via GIT), share progress and get feedback.

Teamwork and Interactions

I have mostly been working alone on the Unity side but I have been collaborating for the SAP side with the SAP developpment team.

Learning Examples

Remaking the whole Game Manager needed me to reimagined of the whole app needed to work. I have made several pages to list every possible actions the new Game Manager needed to do to keep the app running while optimazing and debugging it. It took me several days but at the end, the app was better optimized, less minor bugs, more malleable and easier to debug.
